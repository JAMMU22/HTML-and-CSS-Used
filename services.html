Services

  • Website design

    Like everything else, the price of a website redesign largely comes down to supply and demand. When the internet was still relatively new, businesses didn’t understand how valuable a website really was, so they weren’t willing to pay a high price.

    • Designing a website yourself is the cheapest option. The cost can range anywhere from $100 to $3,000 or more.
    • A simple custom website design from an agency can range from $15,000 to $30,000.
    • Bigger and more complicated websites will cost between $40,000 to $75,000 or more.
